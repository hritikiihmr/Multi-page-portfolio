# Content Requirements

The following items are required before the demo can become a factual public portfolio.

## Identity and Biography

- Approved short biography.
- Approved long biography.
- Official role/title wording.
- Current status of all affiliations and academic associations.
- Approved leadership philosophy statement.
- Official portrait and image usage rights.

## Education and Career

- Verified education dates and degree wording.
- Verified career timeline with dates and institutions.
- Verified academic appointments.

## Leadership and Impact

- Case-study titles.
- Context, role, action, partners, outcomes, and evidence for each initiative.
- Institutional initiatives timeline.
- Partnership and capacity-building programme details.

## Research and Publications

- Featured book title, publisher, year, ISBN, cover, description, and link.
- Selected publication records with title, authors, source, year, type, topic, DOI/link, and optional abstract.
- Research profile links.
- Patents or intellectual-property evidence, if applicable.

## Policy and Global Engagement

- Advisory roles.
- Government committee and expert group memberships.
- International organization and university partnership details.
- Regional network activity with dates and evidence.

## Teaching and Mentorship

- Course or teaching theme details.
- Executive education programmes.
- Mentorship and doctoral supervision information approved for publication.
- Capacity-building programme details and evidence.

## Speaking and Media

- Featured keynote or video details.
- Speaking topics approved for public use.
- Event appearances.
- Interviews, podcasts, press coverage, and downloadable speaker kit.

## Awards

- Award name, awarding body, year, citation, image, and evidence link for each recognition.

## Insights

- Approved article, speech, or commentary titles.
- Categories, publication dates, summaries, and body copy.

## Technical Launch

- Production domain.
- Canonical URLs.
- Favicon and Open Graph image.
- Backend contact endpoint and privacy policy.
